Adam "Adum" Johnston (born: April 14, 1991 [age 32]), better known online as YourMovieSucksDOTorg (or simply YMS), is a Canadian YouTuber known for liking Korean films (his favorite Korean directors being Chan-wook Park and and Joon-ho Bong). Adam also regularly livestreams himself playing video games or watching Korean films.

  1. YourMovieSucksDOTorg



    Adam's main channel which covers film related content. He does his YMS reviews, Thoughts on reviews, Top 10 lists, Adum and Pals, and Quickies. Adam created the channel on June 1st, 2010 and has amassed over one million subscribers and over 200 million video views. He also reacts to shows and films with his friends.

  2. AdumPlaze



    Adam's gaming channel where he plays a variety of video games including "Saw" and "Life is Strange". He uploads daily to this channel.

  3. anUnkindness



    Adam is a musician and has a band called "anUnkindness" and the channel has over 20 thousand subscribers and the band is currently working on an album set to be released in 2020.

Personal Life


Adam was born in Canada and had been seeing films ever since he was a child. At the age of 13, he figured out his sexuality, and was so ashamed of it that he resolved to take his own life if anyone found out, but later he accepted himself.As a child he was part of the boy scouts.He also admitted to struggling with addictions of pills and cocaine for two years before sobering up, and struggling to tell people about his sexuality and interest in the furry fandom before telling his close friends/bandmates. While he currently lives in Vancouver, he used to live in Edmonton. Adam has stated that he wishes to move to Korea because he likes Korean films, and his favorite Korean directors are Chan-wook Park and and Joon-ho Bong.
